What We Do

​Our philanthropy consists of the awareness of domestic violence. Throughout the year, we volunteer at women shelters, including Genesis Women’s Shelter and Hope’s Door. Every year, we host a S.A.F.E. (Stop Abuse in Family Environments) Week in order to promote awareness of domestic violence. During this week, we host a variety of events, some of which include inviting guest speakers, toiletry drives, bake sales, and fun activities that aid in educating others about the prevalence and dangers of domestic violence.
